Volunteering Opportunities

Join our mission to protect and preserve our oceans and coastlines.

Marine Life Protection

Help safeguard endangered marine species by supporting local conservation teams. Volunteers assist with habitat surveys, rescue operations, and community outreach programs.

Location:Maldives, Coastal Regions
Duration:2–8 weeks
Program Fee:From $1,200 (includes accommodation & training)
Activities:Marine species surveys, Turtle hatchery support, Reef monitoring

Beach Clean-Up

Join fellow volunteers in cleaning and restoring coastal beaches. Your efforts help prevent pollution, protect marine life, and raise awareness in local communities.

Location:Bali, Indonesia; Costa Rica; Florida (USA)
Duration:1–4 weeks
Program Fee:From $500 (includes basic accommodation)
Activities:Collecting plastic & debris, Sorting recyclables, Community education

Water Quality Monitoring

Assist environmental scientists in testing and analyzing water samples from rivers, estuaries, and coastal areas. Help track pollution levels and support clean water initiatives.

Location:Various coastal and riverine sites globally
Duration:2–6 weeks
Program Fee:From $900 (includes training & field equipment)
Activities:Collecting water samples, Lab testing, Data reporting & analysis

Coral Reef Restoration

Participate in restoring damaged coral reefs by growing and transplanting healthy coral fragments. Learn about marine ecosystems and help preserve vital underwater habitats.

Location:Great Barrier Reef (Australia), Philippines, Caribbean
Duration:2–12 weeks
Program Fee:From $1,500 (includes diving certification & lodging)
Activities:Coral nursery maintenance, Transplanting coral fragments, Monitoring reef health
